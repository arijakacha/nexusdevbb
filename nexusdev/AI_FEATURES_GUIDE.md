## 🔧 **Troubleshooting:**

### **AI Features Not Showing:**
1. **Check:** Do you have an organization created?
2. **Check:** Are you on the dashboard view?
3. **Check:** Is the cache cleared? `php bin/console cache:clear`

### **API Key Issues:**
1. **Without key:** Still works with templates
2. **With key:** Better quality content
3. **Invalid key:** Falls back to templates

### **Generation Errors:**
1. **Network issues:** Uses fallbacks
2. **API limits:** Uses templates
3. **Any error:** Graceful degradation
